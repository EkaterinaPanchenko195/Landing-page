Chobani is Shaping the Future of Food

  • Community Wellness

    By volunteering our time, donating our products, and supporting impactful charitable programs, we're strengthening our communities and helping those in need.

  • Inclusivity

    At Chobani, we champion diversity, inclusion, equity, and belonging. Diversity and inclusion are a foundational part of our ethos, our policies, and our guiding voice—and will remain as we move on the path forward.

  • Child Hunger

    We believe access to good food is a right, especially for children, which is why we are actively fighting to eradicate child hunger in America, especially in the communities where we operate.

  • Sustainability

    From our supply chain, to people and communities, to corporate responsibility, we’ve been working toward our sustainability goals since 2017. We will never stop exploring new opportunities to improve the world.
